    "At 74, I’ve been remarkably lucky, but not untouched by illness and aging. As a result of a side effect of a life saving drug, I lost my teeth. I decided to try implants at a steep discount dental chain which,after 5 years, failed and required multiple surgeries to remove all 3 of the upper implants. After many consultations with several board certified prosthodontists, I was led to believe new implants were not feasible as the cow bone transplant -necessary when the failed implants were removed - would not support them. One possible option was to place long metal rods up through both sides of my sinus cavity to serve as anchors. Frightening! Needless to say, I reluctantly opted for a traditional upper and snap in lower set of dentures and selected one of the specialists I’d already consulted. Over a year later and after a frustrating series of delays in the delivery, and then multiple adjustments, the teeth never fit properly and were too uncomfortable to wear even just for cosmetic reasons, so I hid behind a mask whenever I went out. I was resigned eating only foods that required little chewing. However, when my GI doctor informed me that the lack of properly chewing my food was exasperating my digestive issues, I started looking for a new prospodontist in hopes of obtaining a workable set of false teeth. Enter Dr. Xavier Saab and Team Somehow, my Guardian Angel led me to Dr Xavier Saab of Houston Prosthodontic Specialists. Reading about Dr. Saab’s extensive training and mainly because of his specialization in challenging dental needs, I decided to make a tentative approach. From my initial contact with Estella, and Doreen, I felt the difference in the priority placed on patient satisfaction and pride in their delivery of that satisfaction. And, that difference was evident in the caring tone and professional attention of Dr. Saab and in each interaction I’ve had with everyone in this outstanding practice over the past 5 months. Fortunately, this level of customer importance, and the trust it engendered, is supported by the top notch skill and expertise of Dr Saab, who is rightfully recognized as a master, as well as a respected teacher, of his profession. His support team also top notch. Dr Saab offered a solution that allowed me to have implants and snap in dentures! I can enjoy salads again! I can even chew a rarely enjoyed medium rare steak:) My teeth are amazingly natural looking and my smile is now non stop! I get compliments and even wear lipstick again (all icing on the cake:) I feel 20 years younger!!! Thank you Dr Saab for your caring treatment and your outstanding expertise! My pearly whites and I are eternally grateful to you and your outstanding team!"
